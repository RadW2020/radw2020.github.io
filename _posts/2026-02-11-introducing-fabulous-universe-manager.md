We are excited to introduce **Fabulous Universe Manager**, a project designed to help social media creators achieve consistency and efficiency. 

Fabulous Universe Manager is a content management tool specifically built for TikTok creators, based on the powerful open-source software **Postiz**. It allows users to:
- **Schedule videos** to ensure they hit the optimal posting times.
- **Manage multiple channels** from a single interface.
- **Track performance** and stay organized with their content calendar.

Our goal is to provide a reliable service that empowers creators to focus on what they do best: creating amazing content. This tool is built with the modern creator in mind, ensuring that managing a digital presence is as seamless as possible.

### Alpha Version & Early Access
We are currently in **Alpha version**, which means we are actively refining the experience. If you are a creator and would like to help us test the tool, we would love to hear from you!

If you want a test account to try out Fabulous Universe Manager, please **get in touch** with us to request access.

Whether you are just starting out or managing a large community, Fabulous Universe Manager provides the tools you need to grow your influence effectively.

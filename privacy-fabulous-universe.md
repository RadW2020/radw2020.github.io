**Last updated: February 5, 2026**

Fabulous Universe ("we", "our") manages this application solely for internal content scheduling and distribution. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal data of our team and the security of our connected accounts.

## 1. Data We Access

Through our integration with the TikTok for Developers platform, we access the minimum necessary data to identify the authorized channels, including Username, Display Name, Profile picture URL and Account identification tokens (OAuth).

## 2. How We Use Your Data

The data and tokens are used strictly for displaying your brand channels within our private internal dashboard at postiz.uliber.com and executing content publishing requests scheduled by our team. We do not share, sell, or rent this data to any third parties.

## 3. Data Storage and Security

All tokens are stored securely on our private servers and encrypted. Access is restricted to authorized Fabulous Universe personnel only.

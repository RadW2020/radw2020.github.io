**Last updated: February 5, 2026**

Fabulous Universe ("we", "our") manages this application solely for user service content scheduling and distribution. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal data of registered users and the security of our connected accounts.

## 1. Data We Access

Through our integration with the TikTok for Developers platform, we access the minimum necessary data to identify the authorized channels, including Username, Display Name, Profile picture URL and Account identification tokens (OAuth).

## 2. How We Use Your Data

The data and tokens are used strictly for displaying your brand channels within our user dashboard at postiz.uliber.com and executing content publishing requests scheduled by registered users. We do not share, sell, or rent this data to any third parties.

## 3. Data Storage and Security

All tokens are stored securely on our private servers and encrypted. Access is restricted to registered users and authorized personnel only.
